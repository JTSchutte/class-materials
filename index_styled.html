About me

I am a senior in a dual degree program. I am studying Political Science and Geography with a minor in Philosophy. I am supposed to graduate in May. Besides school I am a bee keeper! I have my own bee removal business which is how I get all the bees for my hives. As of now I have around 40 hives that are all rescued colonies. I rescue around 100 hives a year, but I can't keep them all so I give a lot of them away to other bee keepers. What I would like to do with my training from school is pursue a career within a governement agency using my GIS skills.

Why I'm taking the course

I am taking this course to learn how to use maps on the internet. I have wanted to incorporate a map onto my own website in which I can track the locations of hives I have removed throughout each season. I would like an interactive map that I can number each removal that is re-hived and then I can link each one to updates of each removal. I think that people would be interested in seeing where the bees that came out of the home ended up, and follow their progress. I also used to dabble with HTML, and I always wanted to learn more so that is another reason I am in this class.
